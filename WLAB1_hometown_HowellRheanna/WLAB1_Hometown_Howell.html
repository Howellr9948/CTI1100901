Hampton, VA USA

Locations

In the commonwealth of Virginia is an independent city known as Hampton.

About

Weather

The weather is humid, sub-tropical, enjoyable during the hot summer months. In winter it becomes mile and crisp and there is moderate rainfall throughout the year.

Population

There is around 140,000 people living in Hampton, VA.

Sports

Hampton has a variety of sports: Baseball, Basketball, Cheerleading, Softball, Football, and Soccer.

Things To Do

  • Public Piers to fish and enjoy water activities
