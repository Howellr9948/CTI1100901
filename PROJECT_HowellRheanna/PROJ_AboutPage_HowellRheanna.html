Developer
The developer of the TypeWrite is Rheanna Howell. She has made a beta program of the website during her second year in college as a project. Rheanna has taken inspiration from websites like Fanfiction.net and Archive of our Own to bring another resource to the fan community to post their stories about their favorite fandoms. The website is ambitious as a single programmer and will remain in the Alpha stage and refined over time before made public. She has created forums that allows writers to post anonymously as a guest until accounts can be created. Forums are available for bug reports and suggestions on making the website more user-friendly for the public.
Intention of Website The intent of TypeWrite is to allow fans to post their own stories and later their own artwork for their fics for others to view, much like Archive of our Own and Fanfiction.net does. As the website continues to grow thanks to input and assistance from other programmers, it will be more accessible to user's to continue posting their own stories for others to view and enjoy.
If you wish to lend your experience and help develop and manage the website, please contact us. Any assistance to help the website grow is appreciated! If you wish to lend your skills, make sure to email your portfolio. This will help us to determine what skills can be used to help grow this website.
